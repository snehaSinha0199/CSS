Symptoms of Covid19

The most common symptoms of covid 19 are:


  • Fever
  • Headache
  • Running Nose
  • dry cough
  • tiredness

It takes atleast 7-8 days to recover from common symptoms after taking precautions on it.


Minor Symptoms:

  • Loss of smell or taste
  • Diarrhea
  • Nasal congestion
  • Conjuctivitis
  • chills or diziness
  • nausea
  • chest pain
  • Muscles or joints pain

It almost takes atleast 15-20 days to recover from minor symptoms after taking proper medicating along with precautions.


Major Symptoms:

  • Breathing difficulty
  • High temperature
  • Rashes
  • lot of Weakness

If you are feeling Major Symptoms you should consult a doctor as soon as possible and should take a healthy and proper diet.


Precautions Measures

If COVID-19 is spreading in your community, stay safe by taking some simple precautions, such as:


  • Social distancing should be maintained.
  • Make sure to wear a N19 mask or double mask.
  • Proper alcohol based hand sanitization if you touch anything
  • Don't go outside without needed.
  • Avoiding Crowded areas.
  • Coughing into a tissue or use and throw materials.
  • Cleaning your hands properly.
  • Don't touch your eyes,nose,mouth.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell.
  • if you feel any symptoms first isolate yourself.
